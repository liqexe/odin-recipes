Hot Chocolate

Description

This hot chocolate with sweetened condensed milk is easy to make. Keep some condensed milk on hand for cold days, and whip up a batch of this rich hot chocolate to warm your loved ones.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) can Eagle Brand sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 6 ½ cups hot water
  • 3 mini marshmallows

Steps

  1. Combine sweetened condensed milk, cocoa, vanilla, and salt in a large saucepan over medium heat; mix well.
  2. Slowly stir in hot water; cook until heated through, stirring occasionally. Do not boil.
  3. Top with marshmallows.
